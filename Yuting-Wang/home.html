Rice

Taiwanese eat rice almost every day for meals. People also use rice to produce wine beer and dessert. It is one of the most popular foods and is used in many dishes.

Noodle

Noodles are an essential ingredient and staple in Taiwanese cuisine. Noodles vary widely according to the region of production, ingredients, shape, and manner of preparation.

Dumpling

Dumplings generally consist of minced meat and finely chopped vegetables wrapped into a piece of dough skin. In Taiwan, dumplings usually refers to boiled dumplings.
